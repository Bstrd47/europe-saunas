# Version 1.0 data-quality audit

Audit date: 12 July 2026  
Scope: all 51 published listings  
Method: current venue websites were preferred. A listing is **verified** when its primary visitor information is supported by a usable current first-party page, **partial** when the venue is confirmed but material facts are missing or conflicting, and **unresolved** when no usable current first-party source was found.

## Outcome

- 35 verified listings
- 13 partial listings
- 3 unresolved listings
- 14 listings received material data corrections
- All 51 records passed structural validation after the audit
- Coordinates remain approximate address points. Exact entrance geocoding is a separate follow-up task.
